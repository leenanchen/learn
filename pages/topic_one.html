Talk 1: My Favorite City

My Favorite City - Harbin

Harbin is the capital of Heilongjiang Province in Northeast China. It is known for its cold climate and winter tourism, with the annual Harbin Ice and Snow Festival attracting visitors from all over the world.

Harbin has several unique and notable features:

1.Ice and Snow Festival: the Harbin Ice and Snow Festival is the largest winter festival in the world. It features massive ice sculptures, snow art exhibits, and various winter sports and activities.

3.Natural beauty: Harbin is located in a beautiful natural setting, with the Songhua River running through the city and scenic parks like Harbin Sun Island and Stalin Park. In winter, the frozen river and snowy landscapes create a stunning winter wonderland.

3.Architecture: Harbin’s architecture combines Chinese, Russian, and European styles, creating a unique blend of East and West. The city’s iconic buildings include the central street, Sophia Square, and the Harbin Grand Theatre.
