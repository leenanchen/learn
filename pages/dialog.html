Is reading a more important way to learn the world than travelling?

  • A: Hello, do you think reading is more important than traveling for learning about the world?
  • B: Hmm, I think both can be fun and educational.
  • A: Yes, I agree. But if you had to choose one, which would you say is more important?
  • B: I think reading is more convenient because you can learn from anywhere and there are lots of books to choose from.
  • A: That’s a good point. But what about the experience of traveling and seeing things for yourself?
  • B: That’s true too, but it can be expensive and not everyone can do it.
  • A: Right, it depends on what you want to learn and how you like to learn.
  • B: Yes, everyone learns differently!

  • B: Yes, I have. I think it would be fun to learn about a new culture and meet new people.
  • A: That’s true. One of the advantages of studying abroad is the opportunity to experience a new way of life.
  • B: Yes, and it can also help me improve my language skills.
  • A: Definitely. However, there are also some disadvantages, like being far away from family and friends.
  • B: Plus, it can be expensive to study abroad.
  • A: Yes, that’s something to consider. But I think the benefits of studying abroad, like personal growth and new experiences, outweigh the costs.
  • B: I agree. It’s a chance to learn about the world and develop independence.
  • A: Exactly. Studying abroad can be an exciting and rewarding experience.
  • B: Yes, it’s definitely worth considering if you want to broaden your horizons.
