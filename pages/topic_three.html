Talk 3: Which is More Important in Finding a Job, Education or Experience?

Both education and experience are important factors in finding a job. However, their importance can vary depending on the specific job and industry.

In some fields, education may be more important, because it provides foundational knowledge and skills in a specific field, Certain jobs and industries also require a specific level of education. while in others, experience may be more valuable. It shows that a candidate can apply their knowledge and skills in practical situation, and demonstrates adaptability and the ability to handle different challenges and situations.
